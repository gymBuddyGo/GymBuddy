About GymBuddy

The GymBuddy system is designed to help College of Staten Island students find gym partners based on their availability and preferences.

The system allows users to:

  • Create and manage accounts
  • Log in securely
  • Select gym session times using an interactive calendar
  • Modify their personal profiles information/preferences
  • Share availability with other users
  • Intuitive matching system based on preferences
  • Have real time chat with other gym buddies
  • Friend management with your buddies

The main goal of GymBuddy is to promote collaboration, motivation, and a sense of community among students by connecting them with gym partners who share similar workout schedules.
